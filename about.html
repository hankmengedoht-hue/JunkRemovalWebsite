How We Started

Our Story

Palmetto Haul Co. started the way most great ideas do — out of necessity. Hank and Jacob were looking for ways to build something meaningful while still in school, something that would let them serve their community and develop real skills along the way.

They noticed that most junk removal companies in Charleston were either overpriced franchises or unreliable one-man operations. They knew they could do it better — show up when they say they will, charge fair prices, and treat every customer's home like it's their own.

So they bought a truck, built this website, and got to work. That's the whole story. No venture funding, no headquarters — just two guys with a work ethic and a genuine love for the Lowcountry.
