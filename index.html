FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your junk removal.

How much does junk removal cost?

Every job is different — price depends on the volume, type, and location of your junk. We come out, take a look, and give you a free upfront quote before we touch anything. No surprises, ever.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! Same-day pickups are often available depending on our schedule. Give us a call or text and we'll do our best to get there today.

What areas do you serve?

All of Greater Charleston — the Peninsula, James Island, Johns Island, Mount Pleasant, Folly Beach, North Charleston, West Ashley, Daniel Island, Sullivan's Island, Isle of Palms, Kiawah, Hanahan, and Edisto Beach. Not sure? Just call.

What items can't you take?

For safety and environmental reasons we can't haul hazardous waste, chemical drums, asbestos, oil/gas tanks, medical waste, or paint cans with liquid paint. Not sure about a specific item? Give us a call and we'll let you know.

How do I pay?

We accept cash, PayPal, Venmo, and check. Payment is due at time of service — and we never charge you until you've approved the price.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Palmetto Haul Co. is a licensed and insured junk removal business operating in Charleston, SC.

Do I need to be home?

In most cases yes — we need someone present to approve the price before we begin. If you have a unique situation, just give us a call and we'll work something out.

How do I get a quote?

The fastest way is to call or text us directly. You can also fill out the booking form on this page and we'll get back to you within an hour during business hours (7am–8pm daily).
